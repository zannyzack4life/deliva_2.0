Jollof Rice Supreme

45 min
4 servings
Easy

The crown jewel of West African cuisine, elevated with Mama Tee's authentic sauce blend. A dish that brings families together and creates lasting memories.

Watch Cooking Tutorial

Master the art of perfect Jollof rice with our step-by-step video guide

üë©‚Äçüç≥

Chef's Secret Tips

  • Rinse rice until water runs clear for fluffy texture
  • Let Mama Tee's sauce bloom in oil for deeper flavor
  • Use the "knuckle method" for perfect water ratio

Ingredients

Jasmine rice 2 cups
Mama Tee's Original Sauce 3 tbsp
Chicken, cut into pieces 1 lb
Large onion, diced 1
Tomatoes, blended 3
Bay leaves 2
Chicken stock 3 cups
Salt and pepper To taste

Instructions

1
Season and cook chicken pieces until golden brown and cooked through
2
Saut√© onions until translucent, then add blended tomatoes and cook until reduced
3
Add Mama Tee's Original Sauce and cook for 5 minutes to develop flavors
4
Add rice, chicken stock, and bay leaves. Bring to a boil
5
Reduce heat and simmer covered for 20 minutes until rice is tender
6
Let rest for 10 minutes before serving. Garnish and enjoy!

Grilling Pro Tips

  • Marinate for at least 2 hours for maximum flavor
  • Preheat grill to avoid sticking
  • Use meat thermometer for perfect doneness

Ingredients

Chicken breasts 4 pieces
Mama Tee's Spicy Sauce 4 tbsp

Instructions

1
Marinate chicken with Mama Tee's Spicy Sauce for 2 hours

4
Grill for 6-7 minutes per side until cooked through
5
Let rest for 5 minutes before serving
6
Garnish with fresh herbs and serve hot

Plantain Pro Tips

  • Use ripe but firm plantains for best texture
  • Don't overcrowd the pan when frying
  • Gentle tossing preserves the plantain shape

Ingredients

Ripe plantains, sliced 4
Mama Tee's Mild Sauce 3 tbsp
Vegetable oil 2 tbsp
Small onion, sliced 1
Salt To taste

Instructions

1
Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat
2
Fry plantain slices until golden on both sides
3
Remove plantains and saut√© onions until soft
4
Add Mama Tee's Mild Sauce and cook for 2 minutes
5
Return plantains to pan and gently toss
6
Serve immediately while hot

Traditional Stew Tips

  • Brown meat properly for rich flavor base
  • Low and slow cooking for tender results
  • Adjust liquid as needed during cooking

Ingredients

Beef, cubed 2 lbs
Mama Tee's Original Sauce 4 tbsp
Large tomatoes, blended 4
Onions, diced 2
Beef stock 4 cups
Spices and seasonings To taste

Instructions

1
Brown beef cubes in a heavy pot
2
Add onions and cook until soft
3
Add blended tomatoes and Mama Tee's Sauce
4
Add beef stock and bring to boil
5
Simmer covered for 1.5 hours until tender
6
Adjust seasoning and serve with rice
